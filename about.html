About Us

Who We Are

The Bajali New Rays Foundation (BNRF) is a non-profit social welfare organization rooted in the Bajali district of Assam, India. Established in 2020, the foundation was born out of a shared belief that every individual deserves access to basic health, education, and social support — regardless of their background or circumstances.
