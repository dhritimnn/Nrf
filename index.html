ABOUT US


The Bajali New Rays Foundation is a social welfare organization based in the Bajali district of Assam, India. The foundation is active in organizing community-driven initiatives focused on health and social welfare.

Learn More...

Recent Activities and Focus Areas



Health Initiatives:
The foundation recently organized a free health check-up camp on December 7, 2025, in Bamakhata, Bajali. This event was held in collaboration with Medivision to provide accessible medical services to the local community.


Social Welfare:
The organization operates under the broader umbrella of social service in the region, often collaborating with other local entities for public welfare programs.
